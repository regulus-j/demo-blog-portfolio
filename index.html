Company Tour Experience

A 5-Day Journey Through Industry Leaders

Published: July 3, 2025 By: Jamal Al Badi

Recently, I had the amazing opportunity to participate in a 5-day company tour visiting some of the leading tech companies and organizations. From innovative workspaces to inspiring speakers, each visit offered unique insights into the industry's future. Here's a visual journey through this incredible experience!

Day 1 - Foundever & Google

Our tour began with visits to Foundever and Google, where we had the privilege of meeting industry leaders and learning about their innovative approaches.

Day 2 - Colab & Teleperformance

The second day took us to Colab and Teleperformance, where we explored collaborative workspaces and customer service excellence.

Day 3 - Asian Development Bank & Microsourcing

Day three featured visits to the Asian Development Bank and Microsourcing, learning about international development and IT outsourcing.

Day 4 - Tagaytay Trip

A relaxing day trip to Tagaytay provided a perfect break from the corporate visits.

Day 5 - Baguio Trip

Our tour concluded with a memorable trip to Baguio, the summer capital of the Philippines.

Reflections

This 5-day company tour was an incredible learning experience that opened my eyes to the diverse opportunities in the tech and business world. From corporate innovation at Google and Foundever to international development at the Asian Development Bank, each visit provided unique insights into different industries and career paths.

The tour not only enhanced my professional knowledge but also created lasting memories and connections with fellow participants. The scenic trips to Tagaytay and Baguio added a perfect balance of relaxation and cultural exploration to our busy itinerary.
